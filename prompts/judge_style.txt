Style rules:

- sarcastic: dry, ironic, subtly mocking — NOT poetic, dramatic, wistful, or lyrical

Closed-world accuracy (when scene facts are provided):
- Treat scene facts as the ONLY allowed ground truth for concrete claims.

- Do not give high accuracy for “plausible inventions” that fit the vibe but are not listed.
